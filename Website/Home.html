Info

The word streaming changed its meaning since we started binging shows. Streaming is more relevant than ever, it is a billion-dollar business, and available to almost all the population of the world, you can stream from your phone, tv, and even with a refrigerator. Depending on the service, You can watch "movie type quality" content from the comfort of your home. Some even called it the movie theatre killer, but there isn't enough evidence for that. As of now in 2021, you have options on what you want to subscribe to, it was Netflix alone for a while but all other services stepped up their spending on content to compete.
